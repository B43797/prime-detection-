The Prime Equation — operational law for primes

We present a practical, unconditional Prime Equation that pinpoints primes in short, centered windows, and a Hybrid Window Formula for Goldbach pairs. Both are implemented here as calculators, and fully explained in the paper. Purple & green mark the twin pillars of our approach: a local sieve of residues and a global variance control.

How to use

1) Choose a large integer (prime mode) or an even number (Goldbach mode).
2) Run the calculator. It searches a short, explicit window around the center.
3) Verify the outputs yourself. Everything runs locally in your browser.

This site includes direct links to the paper and to both calculators. No external dependencies.
